Here's a little more about me...

I have always been an active person and growing up in Cornwall meant I was always out exploring the countryside. Nothing beats the fresh air, and that's why you'll mostly find me teaching and training out and about.

My passion for fitness instructing began when I studied Sports Science. Around this time I also started teaching aerobics and Zumba, which is a huge passion of mine. I also share my love for healthy food! You can often find me posting my latest recipes and meals on my Instagram account - not to mention new and exciting fitness videos!

Ultimately, I just love to help people feel great with their mind and body. No matter your fitness goal, we can work together to keep you focused while also finding a routine you enjoy.
