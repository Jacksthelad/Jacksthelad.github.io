Personal Training

Exercise and fresh air - this combination will make you happy and energised! Functional training helps provide you with flexibility, stability, power, mobility, endurance and strength. We will use basic functional movements like pushing, pulling, hinging, squatting, rotating, carrying, walking and running to work towards your goals. I believe personal training works best outside, making you feel energised and easier to kick those stresses away. I'm here to make sure you achieve your goals safely and with good health, so let's get started!

Group Classes

Join me in group classes to have fun, move and be a healthy you. My classes are high impact, certain to make a difference but they're never without a good beat - certain to keep you motivated as a group!
